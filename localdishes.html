KUNEFE

It's been said that kunefe's origin from Syria and Lebanese but its homeland is the southern of Turkey, especially the city of Hatay. Kunefe is a desert, made of shredded wheat and cheese, served hot. It's cooked in various ways in the city of Sanliurfa, Hatay and Mersin.
